ARMAS SOBRE O JOGO SOBRE A PÁGINA
Dagger

A standard dagger with a straight blade.

Though modest in reach and capacity for harm, this weapon is light enough to jab in rapid succession and delivers devastating critical hits.

Parrying Dagger

A knife with curved handguards. Designed to parry, turning foes' attacks against them.

For master of combat who anticipate every enemy strike and counter accordingly, this weapon is all they need.

Wakizashi

Short sword with a tempering pattern on the blade.

A weapon unique to warriors of the Land of Reeds.

Afflicts targets with blood loss.

Bloodstained Dagger

Dagger with a bloodstained blade.

Afflicts targets with blood loss.

Great Knife

Large knife with a broad, curved blade.

This weapon is designed to slash and rend, inflicting blood loss upon the recipient.

Erdsteel Dagger

An erdsteel dagger with a grass crest engraved upon its blade.

Carried by the Erdtree royalty for self-defense in times of peace.

Main Gauche

Dagger with a large hand guard.

Intended to be used in the left hand, thereby complementing a piercing sword or identical blade held in the right to enable a distinctive style of fighting.

Misericorde

Dagger favored by military physicians in white.

The pointed blade is hard and sharp, making critical hits especially potent.

Fireknight Shortsword

Shortsword used by the Fire Knights.

A slender blade that undulates like fire. Blessed with a flame incantation.

Further flame imbuement will amplify the effect and greatly increase the armament's power.

Blade of Calling

Dagger given to one who set out on a journey to fulfill her duty long ago.

The power of its former owner, the kindling maiden, is still apparent.

Black Knife

Dagger once belonging to one of the assassins who murdered Godwyn the Golden on the Night of the Black Knives.

A ritual performed on the oddly misshapen blade imbued it with the power of the stolen Rune of Death.

Ivory Sickle

Sickle fashioned from ivory.

Weapon carried by aged Albunaurics.

Celebrant Sickle

Sickle decorated with flowers and many-coloured fabrics.

Ceremonial tool used by dancers during the festivities of Dominula.

Glintstone Kris

Ritual blade once presented to Leyndell by the Academy of Raya Lucaria to celebrate their newfound peace.

Though the weapon is embedded with precious glintstones and features Erdtree ornamentation, the undulating blade is symbolic of an ancient ritual.

Crystal Knife

A knife made by sharpening hard crystal.

Signature weapon of glintstone miners.

Scorpion Stinger

Dagger fashioned from a great scorpion's tail, glistening with scarlet rot.

A ceremonial tool used by heretics, crafted from the relic of a sealed outer god.

Reduvia

Jagged dagger with a distinctive curled blade.

Carried by the noble servants of the Lord of Blood.

Cinquedea

Short sword given to high ranking clergymen of Farum Azula.

Raises potency of bestial incantations.
